## Prompts Used
1. I started out by asking for help identifying shared resources and concurrency issues in the given code.
2. I asked for clarification on how different synchronization fixes affect thread safety.

## How AI Helped
AI helped me understand the concurrency issues in the code, especially how race conditions can occur with shared variables and collections. It also guided me in evaluating different synchronization approaches and understanding why some fixes were incomplete. Additionally, it helped connect the problem to object-oriented design principles, which made my answers more structured and accurate.

## Reflection
Using AI helped me break down a complex problem into smaller parts and understand each concept more clearly. I made sure to review the explanations and ensure I understood why each answer was correct, rather than just copying the responses.
